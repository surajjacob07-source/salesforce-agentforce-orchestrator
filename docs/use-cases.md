# Use Cases

## 1. Lead prioritization
The orchestrator routes account signals to the lead-generation and predictive-impact agents, then recommends whether an account should be prioritized, nurtured, or reviewed manually.

## 2. Sales operations acceleration
When required inputs are available, the orchestrator invokes the order-form creation path to reduce manual back-and-forth and improve execution speed.

## 3. Profitability protection
Accounts showing low profitability or unhealthy credit utilization can be flagged early, reducing delayed discovery and reactive firefighting.

## 4. Compliance-aware execution
Before downstream actions complete, the compliance agent can flag or block actions that violate business rules or thresholds.

## 5. Predictive portfolio management
The predictive-impact path supports earlier identification of future account opportunity or risk, allowing teams to take action before outcomes become obvious in lagging reports.
